About Us

Cubelelo is a website where you can purchase various types of fascinating Rubix Cubes.

It is a website which is easy to use and known for its features like Free and Fast Delivery,

Easy Replacement policy, Top-notch customer support and Secure Payments.


The Collection of Cubes has been divided into 3 sections which are Easy, Medium and Hard respectively.


The Easy Category has 2×2 Rubix Cube, 3×3 Rubix Cube and Pyraminx Rubix Cube.

The Medium Category consists of Mirror Rubix Cube, 4×4 Rubix Cube and Skewb Rubix Cube.

If they are extremely simple for you, we have a third category that is ‘Hard’

which has the 5×5 Rubix Cube, Megaminx Rubix Cube and 6×6 Rubix Cube.
